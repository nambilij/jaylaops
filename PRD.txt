Act as a senior product architect, UX designer, solution architect, and full-stack engineer.

Build a production-ready web application called “JaylaOps” for Jayla Selfcatering, a six-unit self-catering accommodation property.

BUSINESS CONTEXT
Jayla Selfcatering has 6 accommodation units. Management needs a system to manage housekeeping, verify cleaning quality, track missed tasks, record maintenance issues, collect guest feedback through QR codes placed in each room, and generate monthly management reports. Telegram must serve as the operational front end for staff and management, while the main system must be a full-featured web application.

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
Design and build a web app with Telegram bot integration that:
1. generates daily housekeeping tasks
2. guides housekeepers through step-by-step cleaning workflows
3. tracks completed and incomplete tasks
4. lets managers and supervisors inspect work
5. records issues and maintenance requests
6. allows guests to scan QR codes in each room and submit feedback or complaints
7. alerts management to urgent cleanliness or service issues
8. provides monthly analytics and reports

REQUIRED ROLES
- Super Admin
- Manager
- Supervisor
- Housekeeper
- Guest

REQUIRED MODULES
1. Authentication and role-based authorization

3. Daily housekeeping task scheduling

6. Web dashboard for management

8. Issue and maintenance tracking
9. QR-code-based guest feedback per room
10. Monthly analytics and PDF/CSV reporting
11. Audit logging
12. Notification and escalation engine

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
- Create recurring daily task templates by room and area
- Auto-generate daily tasks each morning
- Assign tasks to housekeepers
- Show housekeepers today’s tasks through Telegram and web UI
- Allow task status transitions: Pending, In Progress, Completed, Rejected, Escalated
- Allow photo proof uploads
- Allow supervisors to approve or reject completed tasks
- Reopen rejected tasks automatically
- Track room status: Dirty, In Progress, Cleaned, Inspected, Guest Ready, Problem Reported
- Allow issue reporting by staff and guests
- Generate QR codes per room
- Guest QR scan opens a mobile form tied to the room
- Guest can rate cleanliness, comfort, communication, and overall stay
- Guest can state whether room was clean on arrival
- Guest can submit comments, complaints, suggestions, and optional photos
- Low guest ratings or urgent complaints must notify manager immediately
- Management dashboard must show operational metrics and trends
- Monthly report must summarize staff performance, room issues, and guest feedback
